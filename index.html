Adventure Awaits

You are the only remaining Number Knight, and it is up to you to explore the deep, dark dungeons below, and rescue your fellow Knights.

  • Evade traps!
  • Slay monsters!

  • Defeat bosses!

  • Save the world!

Dungeons & Math

Each dungeon features randomized traps, columns for cover, enemies, and math problems. Test your skills with 3 different difficulty levels for both Math and Monsters.


Game Modes:

  • Multiples
  • Factors
  • Prime Numbers
  • Equality
  • Ascending Order
  • Descending Order
